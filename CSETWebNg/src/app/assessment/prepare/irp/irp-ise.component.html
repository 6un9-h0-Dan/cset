Exam Profile

The Exam Profile evaluates risk areas for your examination. It is measured on a scale from 1 to 5 from least risk to most risk. The exam profile will evaluate into one of these three categories:

1. SCUEP - Low risk. Used by small credit unions, or for a brief overview of security.
2. CORE - Medium risk. The standard risk level and question set to evaluate a credit union.
3. CORE+ - High risk. Evaluates credit unions based on extra measures beyond the standard procedures.
